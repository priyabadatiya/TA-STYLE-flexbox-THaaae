Why we are doing AltCampus?

AltCampus is an alternative to college that you wish existed. Currently we are offering an in-house, 6-monh long program to train people in quality software development, where they don't have to pay anything until they are hired.

We live in the world of digital skills. These skills are evolving and so is their demand, Students/Professionals who are not responsive to the changes in industry demands, in terms of skills. will have a hard time surviving, well at least flourishing.
